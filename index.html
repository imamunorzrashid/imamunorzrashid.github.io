Research profile

I am a final-year B.Sc. student in Mechatronics Engineering at Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET), currently ranked 2nd among 58 students with a CGPA of 3.89/4.00.

My research interests lie at the intersection of power systems, renewable energy, distributed control, and data-driven methods for resilient operation of networked energy systems. My current research investigates frequency control and cyber resilience in isolated and interconnected microgrids under limited training data, communication noise, packet loss, and false data injection attacks.

My broader interests include smart grids, cyber-physical power systems, energy management, and physics-informed and data-efficient machine learning for power and energy applications. I am seeking PhD opportunities where I can contribute to reliable, resilient, and intelligent control strategies for future energy systems.

RESEARCH EXPERIENCE

Research projects and ongoing work

01

Published Research · ECM:X · 2026

Domain-Guided Intelligent Distributed Control for Multi-Microgrid Frequency Resilience

A domain adaptation-aided intelligent distributed control framework for enhancing frequency resilience when target-domain measurements are noisy and high-quality training data are limited.

Research Challenge

Data-driven frequency controllers can degrade when operating conditions change or high-fidelity target-domain data are scarce.

Methodology

A two-phase adaptation strategy first freezes source input-to-hidden weights, then fine-tunes the complete network using limited noisy target samples.

Validation

Evaluated on a two-area interconnected load-frequency-control model of multi-microgrids, with real-time validation on OPAL-RT OP5700.

Thesis & Ongoing Manuscript

Cyber-Resilient Frequency Control of Networked Microgrids

A fully distributed, data-driven attack detection and mitigation framework for communication-dependent microgrid control under false data injection attacks, composite noise, packet loss, and limited training data.
